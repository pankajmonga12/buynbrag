Selling with BuynBrag

BuynBrag is a marketplace of unique, curated finds from across the country. We troll the bylanes of towns small and big, in the search for things that are crafted with love and tell a story.

We invite the creators of these finds to set up shop with us and make it available to others who share our sense of aesthetic, who would appreciate it as much as us.

You can set up a store on BuynBrag if you are one of the following:

  • You have a boutique store
  • You have an e-commerce website
  • You supply products to various stores
  • You help artisans and designers sell their products

The BuynBrag Advantage:

At Buynbrag, we believe in providing you the complete solution for taking your business online. To sell online, you do not have to set up a website, you just need an e-store. We provide you that and lots more and you do what you do best, i.e. create amazing products.

What's more, you sell your creations under your own brand across the country to discerning shoppers who seek quality craftsmanship and unique design.

When an order comes in, we pick it up from your warehouse and have it delivered at the shopper's doorstep.

Therefore we take over complete responsibility for:
  • Designing your Virtual Storefront
  • Product Photography (optional)
  • Content Writing (optional)
  • Inventory Planning
  • Pricing
  • Store Management
  • Logistics Support
  • Customer Care
  • Marketing and Promotions

5. Keyword Spamming Policy
5.1 Keyword spamming occurs when Sellers place brand names or other inappropriate keywords in a title or description for the purpose of gaining attention or diverting members to a listing. Keyword spamming in listings is not permitted on BuynBrag.com. The text sellers place in listings must be directly relevant to the item being sold.
5.2 Violations of this policy may result in a range of actions, including:
• Product Listing cancellation
• Limits on account privileges
• Account suspension
• Loss of Top Rated Seller status at BuynBrag.com
6. Links Policy
6.1 In general, when listing items on BuynBrag.com, Sellers can include links that are solely intended to provide additional details about the items. That means links should only be used in circumstances when that information can’t be included in the listing itself. These links include additional images, technical specifications, manufacturer warranties, and other information that can help buyers make an informed purchasing decision. Other links that point to a third-party provider’s Website are also allowed, such as image hosting sites, payment processors or courier companies.
6.2 BuynBrag.com doesn’t allow links to non−BuynBrag sites or content where other items are offered for sale. BuynBrag continually reviews links on the site and reserves the right to block any link considered unsafe for our members. As a Seller, please make sure that your listing follows links policy of BuynBrag. If it doesn’t, it may be removed, and you may be subject to a range of other actions, including limits your selling privileges and/or suspension of your account.
7. Rules about Intellectual Property
7.1 BuynBrag is committed to protecting the intellectual property rights of third parties and to providing a safe marketplace to trade. Unauthorized use or Infringement of copyrighted material and trademarks are strictly prohibited at BuynBrag.
7.2 BuynBrag has zero tolerance for any intellectual property violations. Counterfeits, unauthorized replicas, unauthorized items (such as counterfeit watches, handbags, or other accessories) or unauthorized copies (such as copies of software programs, video games, music albums, movies, television programs, or photographs) are not permitted at BuynBrag. Unauthorized copies include (but are not limited to) copies that are pirated, duplicated, backed−up or bootlegged. It is illegal to sell unauthorized copies of media.
7.3 If the product you are selling bears the name or logo of a company, but it wasn’t made or endorsed by that company, don’t list it at BuynBrag.
7.4 Academic software is software sold at significantly discounted prices to students, faculty members and educational institutions. Do not list academic copies of software At BuynBrag unless you are authorized to do so as an educational reseller, an educational institution a student or a faculty member.
7.5 Beta software, test software and evaluation software are not permitted at BuynBrag. Beta, test, and evaluation copies of software are preliminary copies of software distributed by software developers strictly for the purpose of evaluation and troubleshooting.
7.6 Do not list "OEM" or "bundled" copies of software At BuynBrag unless you are selling it with computer hardware. For Context, Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM), or bundled software, is software that is obtained as part of the purchase of a new computer. OEM software licenses usually prohibit the purchaser from reselling the software without the computer or, in some cases, without any computer hardware.
7.7 Bootleg recordings are unauthorized recordings of concerts or live performances. Sellers are not permitted to list bootleg recordings at BuynBrag.
7.8 Violations of this policy may result in a range of actions, including:
• Product Listing cancellation
• Limits on account privileges
• Account suspension
• Loss of Top Rated Seller status at BuynBrag.com
8. Pricing Policy
8.1 Please note that trust is the most important aspect of trade between buyers and sellers in marketplace and one of the key aspects of trust is fair pricing. For healthy transaction flow at BuynBrag.com, we ask you to price your items responsibly.
8.2 Please note that we are constantly monitoring prices for each Seller at various channels and do not allow you to price your items at higher prices at BuynBrag.com vs. other channels such as your own retail store, your own Website (if any) or any other 3rd party retail stores or Websites. You should price your items sold at BuynBrag.com to be same as the lowest rate you are selling at elsewhere or even lower but it should not be more. Violation of our pricing policy will result into warning notices and can also result into account suspension and/or store suspension.
9. Honesty in MRP
9.1 Please note the trust you build with your buyers will go away if you artificially start showing higher MRP, so that the selling price appears to be much better deal vs. what it actually is. Artificially increasing MRP is strictly prohibited at BuynBrag.com and any violation will result into account suspension.
