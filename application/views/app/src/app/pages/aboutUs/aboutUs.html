About Us

BuynBrag is the brainchild of Prithvi and Ayush, IIT-IIM graduates who couldn't shake their love of aesthetic, despite the rigours of engineering. A chance conversation turned into an idea that kept them both fascinated in building it for nearly two years, before BuynBrag was finally launched in 2012.

Along the way, they've picked up other kindred spirits who share their mutual fascination and the team currently works out of a much-loved hole-in-the-wall in New Delhi.

At BuynBrag, we love beauty that's slightly off-center, the little imperfections that lend character.

We feel endlessly fascinated by the unique whorls of natural
wood and the layers of graphic art. We love surrounding ourselves
with these things that look like they have a story
about them. And making those stories a part of ours.
And it makes us very happy to share our discoveries with you.

From new products by brands you already love, to beautiful products by brands that
we know you'll love (once you meet them),
BuynBrag is your destination for discovery.

And what good is discovery if you have nobody to share it with (and squeal joyously, if you're so inclined)?
So we've built in stuff like:

Brag Lists: If you love it, 'crown' it! Curate your own brag list and share it with your friends. If your list is popular, your rank is higher.

Brag: Brag about what you buy with your friends on Facebook or Twitter.

Badges: We feel you shouldn't be shy about your fetishes. Earn BragBucks by unlocking badges when you brag, buy or brag products.
